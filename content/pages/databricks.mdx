## Databricks Service Areas

## Unified Data & AI Platform

We implement Databricks as a unified platform across data engineering, analytics, data science, and machine learning. By combining data lake flexibility with warehouse-style performance controls, we reduce architecture complexity and improve delivery speed.

## Data Engineering & ETL Pipelines

Our team builds robust pipelines using Apache Spark on Databricks. These pipelines support ingestion, transformation, and quality enforcement for structured and unstructured data at enterprise scale. Apache Spark remains one of the most adopted distributed data engines in production analytics stacks ([Apache Spark](https://spark.apache.org/)).

## Advanced Analytics & Business Intelligence

We enable SQL analytics on large datasets and integrate Databricks with BI platforms to support trusted business reporting. This gives stakeholders faster access to operational and strategic insights from a governed source of truth.

## Machine Learning & AI Solutions

Our Databricks machine learning services support the full ML lifecycle, from feature engineering to deployment with MLflow controls.
