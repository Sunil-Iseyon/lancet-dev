## Power BI Service Areas

**What is Power BI?** Power BI is a business intelligence platform by Microsoft that helps organizations turn raw data into interactive dashboards and reports. It connects to hundreds of data sources and supports real-time analytics.

## Unified Reporting and Dashboards

Lancet Software India designs Power BI dashboards that aim to centralize business KPIs and deliver role-based visibility for leadership and operational teams. These dashboards consolidate data from multiple sources into a single governed view, enabling executives to monitor performance trends and operational managers to drill into specific metrics. By implementing standardized dashboard templates and consistent design patterns, Lancet reduces the time to deploy new reports and improves analytical consistency across business units.

## Data Connectivity and Modeling

Power BI connects to cloud apps, files, and databases. Lancet Software India builds robust semantic models so business users can explore trusted metrics without manual reconciliations. The team designs data models that support complex relationships between fact and dimension tables, applies row-level security for multi-tenant environments, and implements incremental refresh strategies to optimize data processing. These best practices reduce data preparation time and improve query performance for end users.

## Advanced Analytics Enablement

Lancet Software India supports analysts with DAX modeling and reusable calculations. Report design patterns improve analytical depth and maintainability. The team creates calculation groups, measure tables, and time intelligence functions that analysts can reuse across reports. This approach reduces formula duplication and ensures consistent metric definitions throughout the organization.

## Governance and Enterprise Deployment

Lancet Software India implements workspace governance, refresh policies, gateway design, and access controls. These controls keep reporting secure and auditable. The governance framework includes content certification workflows, deployment pipelines for promoting changes from development to production, and activity monitoring dashboards that track usage patterns and identify optimization opportunities.
